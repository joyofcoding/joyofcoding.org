Unravelling the Journey of a Girl into Coding

A little girl’s journey from being a ballet dancer, competitive gymnast, and watching princess movies to the world of coding. How it happened, what she did, what she went through, the challenges, the triumphs, and where she stands today will be shared with the audience. The message will also give some input, tips, and advice to budding coders, or those who have started the journey and have entered into a plateau phase. It’s based on a true story.

Avanti Sharma

Avanti stands for the Sustainable Development Goals and encourages iGens to play a role in them. Starting with her TEDx about “How an iGen would implement the Sustainable Development Goals”, Avanti has been presenting her ideas on international forums. Prominently, Mobile World Congress Barcelona 2024, Digital Education Stakeholder Forum 2022 hosted by the European Commission, Microsoft Accelerator 2017, Deloitte Horizon 10th Edition 2021, Economic Times Global Business Summit 2023, 2019, Economic Times Women In Tech 2021, KPMG Diversity Summit 2018, and leading technology organizers such as Codemotion 2023, Campus Italia 2018, ICT Spring 2021, WomenInTech 2018, 2020, 2022. Avanti’s contribution has been recognized by Technology Playmaker Awards 2018 hosted by Booking.com, IT Ladies Night 2019, InspiringFifty Belgium 2022, ShEU LEADS (European Commission) 2022, GirlsInTech 2022. Avanti was interviewed by Randi Zuckerberg Influencer, CEO & Founder of HUG and former marketing director, Facebook, for her podcast ‘Dot Complicated’ in 2018. She has been featured in Tech Talks podcast by legendary David Savage, Group Technology Evangelist, Nash Squared in 2017. In 2022, Avanti featured in EU Code Week podcast. Earlier last year, Avanti won the ‘honorable award’ and 1st place for International Delegates at the Largest Science Exhibition in Portugal for a software Studified™ she developed. She was also honored by receiving the ‘Aspiring Teen Award’ at the Women In Tech Europe Awards and representing Europe as a finalist for the Women In Tech Global Awards. Avanti looks forward to bringing more to the Joy of Coding.
