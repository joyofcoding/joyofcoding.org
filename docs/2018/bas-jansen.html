Lessons learned from my daughter during our learn to code journey - Ella & Bas Jansen

13:30 - 14:30 lightning talk

The last four years, since my daughter became eight we participated together in several learn-to-code projects. I thought that I was teaching her something, but actually, she taught me a thing or two. Join us both for a while on our journey during this five minute lightning talk.

About the speakers
