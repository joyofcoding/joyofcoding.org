Speaker instructions

Practical information for Joy of Coding 2017 speakers.

Please see the attendee instructions for most of the information about the conference.

Arrival in Rotterdam

Hotel address: Rotterdam Marriott Hotel, Weena 686, 3012CN Rotterdam

Speakers’ dinner

Speakers can meet each other before the pre-party for dinner at a Thai restaurant, 17:30-19:00. We’ll arrange taxis from the hotel.

Address: De Kleine Ondeugd, Oostzeedijk 348, Rotterdam, next door to the pre-party venue.

Filming

InfoQ will be filming the presentations to put online. Sign the video release form at the registration desk, when you collect your name badge.

Presentations

The projector has 16:9 format and an HDMI connector.

Lightning talks

Send us your slides in PDF format in advance. We’ll combine these into a single presentation, which we’ll display on our laptop.

Lightning talks will be strictly limited to five minutes, with a visible timer.

Speakers will have a handheld microphone and a clicker.
