Daniel Stenberg

Daniel Stenberg is the founder and lead developer of the curl project. He is a Swedish Open Source software developer, actively participating in several networking related projects. He has been involved in Open Source for thirty years, been involved in the IETF and protocol standards for fifteen years. He was awarded the Polhem Prize in 2017.
