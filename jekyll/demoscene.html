Demoscene

The Demoscene is an underground computer art culture. The term demoscene comes from the word demo, short for demonstration which means a realtime audiovisual application demonstrating the capabilities of the machine it runs on. Since 2023 the Demoscene is 'UNESCO cultural heritage' in the Netherlands, so a closer look is long overdue.
